About Me

Where data meets
people & purpose

Hi, I'm Minji! I work at the intersection of people, technology, and information, creating well-designed systems that opens doors for. My focus is on building solutions that promote equitable access to resources, with particular attention to communities that have historically been underserved or excluded.

I completed my BS in Human-Centered Data Science at UT Austin's iSchool, and I'm currently pursuing an MS in Business Analytics at McCombs. Alongside my studies, I serve as an AI Systems & Data Automations Fellow at VIPC, where I work on deploying AI tools that reduce friction and expand opportunity within Virginia's innovation ecosystem.

Responsible AI and human-centered design are at the core of everything I do. I believe technology should be accountable, inclusive, and built with the communities it serves — not just for them. Whether it's data pipelines, automated systems, or analytical tools, I focus on who it will serve, and who it might leave behind?
