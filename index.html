About

About Me

Hello, I'm Minji! I work at the intersection of people, technology, and information, dedicated to create systems and designs that work for people.

My focus is on building solutions that promote equitable access to resources, with particular attention to communities that have historically been underserved or excluded.

I completed my BS in Human-Centered Data Science at UT Austin's iSchool, and now I am serving as an AI Systems & Data Automations Fellow at VIPC, where I deploy AI tools that reduce friction and expand opportunity within Virginia's innovation ecosystem.

Responsible AI and human-centered design are at the core of everything I do. I believe technology should be accountable, inclusive, and built with the communities it serves. Whether it's data pipelines, automated systems, or analytical tools, I am commited to analyzing who this will serve, and who it could leave behind.
