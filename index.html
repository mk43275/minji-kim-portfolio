About Me

Where data meets
people & purpose

Hi, I'm Minji! I work at the intersection of people, technology, and information — driven by the belief that well-designed systems can open doors, not close them. My focus is on building solutions that promote equitable access to resources, with particular attention to communities that have historically been underserved or excluded.

I completed my BS in Human-Centered Data Science at UT Austin's iSchool, and I'm currently pursuing an MS in Business Analytics at McCombs. Alongside my studies, I serve as an AI Systems & Data Automations Fellow at VIPC, where I deploy AI tools that reduce friction and expand opportunity within Virginia's innovation ecosystem.

Responsible AI and human-centered design are at the core of everything I do. I believe technology should be accountable, inclusive, and built with the communities it serves — not just for them. Whether it's data pipelines, automated systems, or analytical tools, I ask: who does this serve, and who might it leave behind?
