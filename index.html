About Me

Where data meets
people & purpose

Hi! I'm Minji — a data scientist and AI fellow with a passion for building systems that are as intelligent as they are human-centered. I completed my undergraduate studies in Human-Centered Data Science at the University of Texas at Austin, where I developed a foundation in research design, data analysis, and user-focused thinking.

I'm currently pursuing an MS in Business Analytics at UT Austin's McCombs School of Business, while working as an AI Systems & Data Automations Fellow at the Virginia Innovation Partnership Corporation (VIPC) — where I help organizations harness AI to streamline workflows and unlock insights.

My work sits at the intersection of analytics, automation, and human experience. I'm drawn to questions about how AI can be designed and deployed responsibly — and how data storytelling can drive real decisions.
